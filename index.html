Notice & Methodology Scope: This browser-based application functions strictly as a semi-quantitative analyzer designed for the localized estimation of total iron ions via recorded RGB colorimetric values. It is optimized as a lightweight supporting tool for rapid point-of-care screen monitoring. For absolute certified analytical precision and full empirical quantification, high-tier validated laboratory instrumentation (e.g., ICP-MS, Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy) remains strongly recommended.
